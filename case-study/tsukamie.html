Tsukamie Noodle Bar is an authentic Japanese restaurant that needed to boost brand awareness and sales in the highly competitive F&B market of Bandung. They had great food but lacked a cohesive digital presence.

My approach was to implement a "Visual Storytelling" strategy. By overhauling their Instagram feed design and focusing on high-contrast food photography and short-form ASMR videos, we successfully translated the rich texture and taste of their noodles into engaging digital content—driving massive viral reach and foot traffic.

The Challenge
[01]
F&B Market Saturation

Fierce competition in Bandung meant standard food photos were ignored. We needed a unique, mouth-watering visual identity to stand out.

[02]
Algorithmic Shifts

Static posts alone couldn't reach new audiences. The brand needed a transition to short-form video (TikTok/Reels) to satisfy the algorithm.

[03]
Visual Inconsistency

The existing social media grid lacked cohesive branding, making the restaurant appear less premium than its actual food quality.

"Appetite begins with the eyes. We built a visual strategy that translates taste into digital engagement."

The Solution

[01]

Texture-Focused Videography

We produced high-energy, short-form videos specifically designed for TikTok and Instagram Reels. By utilizing macro lenses, dynamic camera movements, and ASMR sound design, we triggered immediate cravings and maximized shareability.

0:00/0:00
[02]

Curated Feed Architecture

To establish a premium brand identity, we overhauled the Instagram grid. We implemented a continuous layout strategy combining minimalist typography, lifestyle shots, and high-contrast macro food photography.

[03]

Community-Driven Campaigns

Beyond visual aesthetics, we designed interactive content pillars, including structured giveaways and 'behind-the-kitchen' stories. This transformed passive viewers into active advocates, driving significant offline foot traffic to the store.

Key Takeaways

Working with Tsukamie proved that in the F&B industry, aesthetics and strategy must go hand-in-hand. Beautiful photos are useless without an algorithmic distribution plan (like TikTok Reels), and viral strategies fall flat if the visual branding isn't premium enough to retain the audience.
