When I joined Torch x Gundam, their digital presence was stagnant. TikTok had been inactive for a month, and Instagram engagement was flat. My task was analyzing 1,000+ customer data points to redefine our content pillars and build a sustainable content system.

Within 3 months, we drove +247K TikTok impressions, revitalized Instagram engagement, and managed 20+ local influencers weekly through this new systemized content strategy.

The Challenge
[01]
Platform Inactivity

The TikTok account was dormant and Instagram suffered from stagnant reach, creating a major visibility gap.
[02]
No Structure

There were no content pillars or a consistent calendar, leading to an irregular and ineffective posting schedule.
[03]
Audience Disconnect

Content formats were outdated and misaligned with current F&B social media trends, failing to resonate with the audience.
"Transforming flat data into a visual language that speaks directly to the Gundam community."

The Solution

[01]

Data-Driven Ad Creatives

We analyzed audience data to produce high-energy, visually-driven campaign ads. This video, for example, was edited with rapid cuts and cinematic color grading to maximize engagement in the first 3 seconds.

0:00/0:00
[02]

Systemized Production Workflow

To ensure consistency, we created a modular editing template. This allowed us to rapidly produce trend-aligned content, like this product walkthrough, while maintaining a high-quality visual signature for the brand.

0:00/0:00
[03]

Engagement-Focused Motion

For promotional content, we leveraged motion graphics to create dynamic, eye-catching stories. This approach increased view duration and click-through rates on limited-time offers by making the information more digestible and visually exciting.

This pivotal internship at Torch x Gundam was where I truly began to build the confidence and practical skills necessary for my future marketing career. It was my first time applying classroom theory to a real business, bridging the gap between academic knowledge and practical application.
