Audience Disconnect

The Solution

Developed a structured content calendar and production workflow to ensure a consistent schedule of high-quality, trend-aligned video content.

Community Engagement

Systematically managed collaborations with 20+ local food influencers weekly, amplifying reach and building social proof.
