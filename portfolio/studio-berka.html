Studio Berka is a forward-thinking creative agency that required a digital home as innovative as their projects. Their previous website was slow, difficult to manage, and failed to reflect their premium branding.

The objective was to design and develop a custom, high-performance website from scratch. By focusing on a clean UI, smooth GSAP micro-interactions, and a lightweight code architecture, we delivered an immersive digital experience that captivates visitors while achieving near-perfect Lighthouse performance scores.

The Challenge
[01]
Performance Bottlenecks

The old platform relied heavily on bloated templates and unoptimized assets, resulting in slow load times and high bounce rates.

[02]
Static User Experience

A creative agency needs a dynamic feel. The previous site lacked interactive feedback, making the portfolio presentation feel stiff and generic.

[03]
Complex Content Management

The internal team struggled to update their growing portfolio due to a rigid and confusing backend infrastructure.

"A great website is the perfect balance between beautiful aesthetics and robust, invisible engineering."

The Solution

[01]

Intentional UI/UX Design

Starting with wireframes in Figma, we established a clean, grid-based layout that prioritizes high-quality visuals and typography. Whitespace was utilized strategically to guide the user's eye and highlight Studio Berka's portfolio.

[02]

Fluid Micro-Interactions

To bring the design to life, we integrated GSAP (GreenSock) for custom scroll-triggered animations and Lenis for buttery-smooth scrolling. This added a premium, "Apple-like" feel without sacrificing page load speed.

0:00/0:00
[03]

Scalable Architecture

We moved away from bloated monolithic templates to a modern, custom HTML/CSS/JS build. By structuring the codebase efficiently and integrating a streamlined CMS, the client can now publish new projects in minutes without touching a single line of code.
