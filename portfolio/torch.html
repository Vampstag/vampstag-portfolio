This project marked the first collaboration between Torch and Mobile Suit Gundam, combining Torch’s travel gear identity with the visual legacy of one of Japan’s most iconic franchises.

"Turning the Torch x Gundam collaboration into a cinematic campaign for the Gundam community."

[01]

Delivering polished cinematic visuals while working within tight production timelines and social media formats.
