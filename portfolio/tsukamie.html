Tsukamie Noodle Bar was a project focused on revitalizing a stagnant F&B digital presence by shifting from hard-selling content toward a more community-driven storytelling approach.

The Challenge
[01]
Stagnant Digital Growth

Inconsistent engagement and declining content performance due to inactive posting and lack of structured content direction.

[02]
Fragmented Identity

The brand lacked a structured content calendar and visual pillars, leading to a disconnected and confusing brand perception.

[03]
Promotion Fatigue

Previous efforts relied entirely on hard-selling tactics, failing to build any long-term emotional resonance with the local community.

"Revitalizing a stagnant F&B digital presence by shifting from hard-selling content toward a more community-driven storytelling approach."

The Solution

[01]

Content Planning & Execution Workflow

Developed a structured workflow for planning, producing, and publishing content to ensure consistency across social media platforms.

Insight Discovery

Audience behavior, trend scan, brand objective

Content Strategy Definition

Goal setting: awareness / engagement / conversion

Content Pillar Mapping

Education / Brand Story / Engagement / Promotion

Idea Development

Angle exploration, hook creation, content framing

Pre-Production Planning

Script, caption direction, visual reference, talent coordination

Production Execution

Photo / video / design asset creation

Post-Production & Optimization

Edit, copy refinement, format adaptation per platform

Scheduling & Distribution

Platform timing, feed consistency, posting execution

Community Response Loop

Comment handling, DM, engagement tracking

Performance Analysis

Reach, engagement, retention, content insight

Iteration Feedback Loop

Insight → refinement for next content cycle

[02]

Content Pillar System

Defined clear content categories to maintain consistency in messaging, balancing brand communication, engagement content, and informational posts.

[03]

Community & Content Support

Supported day-to-day content operations including posting execution, community interaction, and collaboration-based content initiatives to maintain audience engagement.
