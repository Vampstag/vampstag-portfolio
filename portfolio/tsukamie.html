Established in 2022, Tsukamie is a Halal-certified Asian fusion noodle bar in Bandung that positions itself as a lifestyle ecosystem rather than just an F&B brand.

This project focused on reshaping their stagnant digital presence by shifting away from direct promotional content toward a more community-driven and narrative-led approach.

Working closely with the brand's leadership, my role was to execute this new direction. I handled the day-to-day video production, photography, visual design, and social media implementation needed to rebuild consistency and audience connection across platforms.

Duration 3 Months
Role Creative Content Support
Industry F&B / Restaurant
The Challenge
[01]
Stagnant Digital Growth

Inconsistent engagement and declining content performance due to inactive posting and lack of structured content direction.

[02]
Fragmented Identity

The brand lacked a structured content calendar and visual pillars, leading to a disconnected and confusing brand perception.

[03]
Promotion Fatigue

Previous efforts relied entirely on hard-selling tactics, failing to build any long-term emotional resonance with the local community.

"Revitalizing an inactive F&B digital presence through community-driven storytelling, relatable content, and consistent social media execution."

The Solution

[01]

Content Planning & Execution Workflow

A structured content system designed to align planning, production, and publishing across social platforms while maintaining consistency and adaptability over time.

Rather than treating content as isolated outputs, this system frames it as a continuous cycle of decisions, behaviors, and feedback.

Insight Discovery

Understanding audience behavior, cultural signals, and brand objectives as the starting point of all content decisions.

Content Strategy Definition

Translating intent into direction across awareness, engagement, and conversion layers.

Content Pillar Mapping

Defining recurring narrative structures: Education / Brand Story / Engagement / Promotion.

Idea Development

Shaping raw insights into content angles, hooks, and narrative framing.

Pre-Production Planning

Structuring execution through scripts, captions, visual references, and talent coordination.

Production Execution

Creating visual assets across photo, video, and design formats.

Post-Production & Optimization

Refining visuals, copy, and format to match platform behavior.

Scheduling & Distribution

Aligning timing, feed structure, and publishing rhythm across channels.

Community Response Loop

Managing interaction flows through comments, DMs, and audience engagement signals.

Performance Analysis

Interpreting reach, engagement, and retention to understand content behavior.

Iteration Feedback Loop

Turning performance insights into refinements for the next cycle of content decisions.

[02]

Content Workflow Implementation

Implemented a structured content workflow guided by the brand's research and pillars. Managed editorial planning, production, outreach tracking, and publishing to ensure consistent social media output.

Photo 01 — Customer Research

Audience research and qualitative feedback collection.

Photo 02 — Content Pillars

Defining the core content categories and communication direction.

Photo 03 — Editorial Planning

Weekly content scheduling and production planning workflow.

Photo 04 — KOL & Outreach Tracking

Managing creator outreach and collaboration coordination.

Photo 05 — Performance Evaluation

Monitoring engagement and evaluating content performance over time.

[03]

Community & Content Support

Acted as an execution layer between content rollout and audience response, supporting engagement, collaboration-based content, and real-time content interaction.

Behind The Scenes

Key Takeaways

Strong systems enable sustainable creative execution.

Clear planning and structured workflows helped turn content production into a more consistent and manageable process over time.

Relatable storytelling drives stronger audience response.

Content that leaned into narrative and community context consistently performed better than direct promotional messaging.

Consistency builds recognition over time.

A unified direction across visuals and content helped shape a clearer and more memorable digital presence.
