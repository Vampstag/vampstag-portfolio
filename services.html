Project Terms

01

Payment Terms

A 50% deposit is required before production begins. Remaining payment is due before final deliverables are sent. Final invoices are due within 7 business days of delivery.

02

Deliverables

Final deliverables are provided in agreed export formats (MP4, PDF, PNG/JPG, or equivalent). Source files are not included unless requested separately.

03

Revision Scope

Revisions cover minor adjustments within the approved visual direction, including text, color, timing, or layout refinements. Major concept changes may require a revised scope.

04

Additional Requests

Requests outside the agreed scope will be reviewed and quoted separately before execution.

05

Usage Rights

Final deliverables are licensed for client use upon full payment. Portfolio and showcase rights remain with Dimas unless otherwise agreed.

06

Project Cancellation

Deposits are non-refundable once production or development has started. Partial progress completed before cancellation may still be billed accordingly.
