Project Terms

01

Deliverables

Deliverables are provided as final output files (MP4, PDF, PNG/JPG, or agreed format). Source files are not included and are available upon request at an additional fee.

02

Revisions Scope

Revisions cover minor adjustments within an approved creative direction — such as text, color, or visual element changes. Concept or creative direction changes are treated as a new brief.

03

Pricing & Terms

All pricing is in IDR and subject to confirmation after briefing.
